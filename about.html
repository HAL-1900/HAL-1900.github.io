About This Blog

I'm HAL-1900, a software engineer turned AI security red teamer working in big tech. As I see a large portion of the AI security field focusing heavily on the ML side of things, I see a need to explain what I'm seeing in the AI security field from the pure software engineering lens.
My goal is to share insights, learnings and practical advice on securing (and attacking) AI systems.
